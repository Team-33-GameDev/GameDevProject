# Click to Live

**Click to Live** is a rogue-lite incremental clicker game developed in Godot 4.7. Set in a dystopian world, players must fulfill oppressive quotas by clicking under extreme time pressure, balancing high-stress clicking phases with relaxed preparation and meta-progression phases.

---

## About the Game
In *Click to Live*, every click matters. You are trapped in a digital prison where your survival depends on meeting ever-increasing click quotas. Manage your resources, upgrade your autoclickers, and survive the quotas to unlock meta-progression upgrades. The core design pillar is a tension-release cycle inspired by incremental mechanics and atmospheric horror.

### Current Features (MVP v2)
* **Core Loop:** Preparation -> Clicking -> Death.
* **Meta-Progression:** Unlock permanent upgrades and new autoclicker types after each run.
* **Shop System:** Spend your earnings on autoclickers and upgrades.
* **3D Environments:** Navigate between the Main Room, Autoclicker Room, and Big Button Room.

---

## Controls
Left Mosue Button: Click to perform actions / interact with UI.
WASD: Move between rooms.
ESC: Open the Pause Menu.
