**Customer Feedback:**
1. **Core Mechanic Engagement (Critical):** The current clicking is too passive. The customer emphasized the need for physical engagement, risk/reward dynamics, and connection to 3D space, referencing Berry Bury Berry as a prime example of physical interaction creating challenge.
2. **Visual Style:** Advised against directly copying CloverPit's PS2 style. Recommended finding a unique visual identity through color palette and style mixing.
3. **Music Integration (Optional):** Suggested connecting music intensity with timer stages or passive purchases for future development.

**Decisions:**
- MVP v2 approved.
- Sprint 3 focus shifted to core mechanic redesign and visual identity.
- Testing strategy will focus on code-level automated tests for Assignment 4 compliance.

**Action Points:**
- Redesign button interaction to require physical engagement.
- Develop unique visual identity.
- Study Berry Bury Berry for physical interaction mechanics.

**Meeting Outcome:** Customer approved MVP v2 but emphasized that Sprint 3 must focus on making the core clicking mechanic engaging, challenging, and physically interactive.
