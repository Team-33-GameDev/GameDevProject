#### 1. Player Agency & Location Interaction
- **Customer Concern:** First-person camera requires more meaningful interactions beyond simple clicking.
- **Team Response:** Planned features include shop system on opposite wall, rhythm-based clicking, parasites/distractions, and AI Jailer challenges.

#### 2. Market Research Recommendation
**Customer strongly recommended playing "Berry Bury Berry"** (free demo available):
- First-person roguelike incremental game.
- Physical interaction creates engagement (carrying berries to pit).
- Location expands through upgrades (breaking walls reveals bigger rooms).
- Multiple progression paths (berries vs. stars collection).
- Level design works with game design for incremental loop.

#### 3. Genre Classification
- Game sits between "simulator" and "incremental" genres.
- Customer noted trend of "slot machine-like" incremental concepts.
- Emphasis needed on: progression within runs + between runs + mechanic evolution.
