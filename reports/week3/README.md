**Team:** Team 33

## Project Overview
Rogue-lite incremental game in dystopian setting. Player fulfills quotas by clicking under time pressure.

**Status:** MVP v1 partially completed (US-013, US-021 done). Remaining Must Haves deferred to Sprint 2 with customer approval.

## Product Status
Playable prototype: first-person movement, clickable button, score tracking, 3D room with lighting.

**Missing:** Timer, quota system, HUD, preparation/death phases, upgrades, shop.
